The Studio

Design that listens before it speaks.

We believe a room should feel inevitable, as though it could not have been any other way.

For nearly two decades, Imagine Interiors has shaped interiors and exteriors across Dhaka with a single conviction: good design is a conversation between the people who live in a space and the materials that hold it. Every brief begins with how a room is actually used, not how it photographs.

From a private residence in Gulshan to a corporate floor for an international team, our work carries the same restraint, the same attention to joinery, light and the warmth of honest materials.

What we do

A complete studio, from first sketch to final detail.

01

Interior Design

Homes, offices, restaurants and hotels designed end to end, balancing layout, light, material and the way a space is meant to feel.

02

Exterior Design

Facades, entrances and outdoor environments that give a building its first impression and carry the language of the interior outward.

03

Custom Furniture

Pieces drawn and made for a single room, from a dining table to a bespoke lounge chair, crafted with joiners we have worked with for years.

04

Remodeling

Thoughtful renovation of spaces that deserve a second life, preserving what works and rethinking what no longer serves the way you live.

05

Project Analysis

Feasibility, spatial planning and material assessment before a single wall moves, so decisions are made on clarity rather than guesswork.

06

Turnkey Delivery

For clients who want a single point of trust, we manage design, procurement and finishing through to the day you walk in and it is done.

How we work

Four steps, one steady hand.

01

Listen

We begin with how you live and work, your light, your habits, the feeling you want a space to hold.

02

Analyse

Spatial planning, material study and feasibility translate the brief into a confident, costed direction.

03

Design

Layouts, finishes and bespoke pieces are drawn, refined and presented until the room feels inevitable.

04

Deliver

We manage build, finishing and furniture so the handover is the quiet, complete moment it should be.
