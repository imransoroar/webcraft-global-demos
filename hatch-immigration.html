About the firm

Guiding your Canadian immigration journey with clarity and care.

Hatch Immigration Consultancy is an incorporated legal immigration practice operating in accordance with CICC (formerly ICCRC) regulatory requirements, and a proud member of the Canadian Association of Professional Immigration Consultants. Our mission is to create an exceptional experience for every client who chooses Hatch. We help individuals, families and businesses navigate the complex, technical and ever-changing landscape of Canadian immigration law — always focused on your individual needs to make the process smoother, faster and easier.
