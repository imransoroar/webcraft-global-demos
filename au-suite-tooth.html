Dr Shaesta Amin

Dr Amin trained at the University of Sydney and built Suite Tooth on a simple belief: dentistry should feel personal, gentle and genuinely caring. She takes the time to know each patient, to explain things in plain language, and to make even an anxious visit feel manageable.

Whether it is a routine clean or a long-awaited cosmetic change, she treats every smile as something worth looking after for the long run.
