Meet your dentist

Dr Shaesta Amin

Bachelor of Dentistry, University of Sydney

Dr Amin leads Suite Tooth Dental with the belief that good dentistry is mostly about listening. Sydney-trained and genuinely unhurried, she takes the time to understand what each patient needs before anything else begins.

Her care spans the whole family, from a child's first visit at two years of age to the considered, preventative work that keeps adult smiles healthy for life. The aim is simple: fewer surprises, less anxiety and a relationship you can rely on.

"Regular check-ups prevent the need for major work later. Calm, consistent care is always kinder than catching up."

Cover and access

Child Dental Benefits Schedule, here at Suite Tooth.

The Child Dental Benefits Schedule provides eligible children with a benefit of up to $1000 for dental services over a two-year period, covering examinations, x-rays, cleaning, fissure sealing, fillings, root canals and extractions. The schedule is means tested and available at Suite Tooth Dental, in accordance with eligibility and terms and conditions. It is almost never too early to begin healthy six-monthly visits.
