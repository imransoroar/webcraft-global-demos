Skilled migration visas

189190491

One of the most common routes to permanent residence. For applicants with skills in demand by the Australian Government, inviting you to live and work across Australia. We also specialise in state sponsored skilled visas for people who may not otherwise meet the points test.

  • 189 Skilled independent
  • 190 Skilled nominated
  • 491 Skilled work regional (provisional)
  • Skills assessment
  • Points test strategy
2.2

Family visas

820801309100300

The family migration stream offers visas for eligible relatives of Australian citizens and permanent residents seeking to live and work in Australia, temporarily or permanently. Partner, child, parent and other family related options, each prepared to its own evidentiary standard.

  • Partner 820 / 801 / 461 / 309 / 100 / 300
  • Child 102 / 103 / 101 / 802 / 445
  • Parent 804 / 864 / 173 / 143 / 870
  • Other family 836 / 114 / 115 / 116 / 838 / 835
2.3

Employer sponsored visas

482

When an employer cannot find an Australian resident or citizen with the required skills, they can sponsor an overseas worker, onshore or offshore, on a temporary or permanent basis. AMG has the expertise to help Australian businesses employ overseas skilled workers, and supports the Business Skills stream.

  • 482 Skills in demand
  • Business sponsorship
  • Employer nominated sponsorship
  • Permanent sponsorship
2.4

Student visas

500

For students coming to Australia to study at a registered institution. You can stay for the duration of your course, and your partner and children can live and work in Australia. International course fees are expensive, so applying without the right advice can prove costly. Get it right before you lodge.

  • 500 Student visa
  • OzzieLand study division
  • Course and institution selection
  • Pathway planning to skilled visas

Dedicated study division: OzzieLand, guiding students from course choice to settling in.
