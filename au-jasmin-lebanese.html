Our family . Our story

A family kitchen on Civic Road

Jasmin1 is locally owned and family run, born from a simple wish: to bring the vibrant flavours of authentic Lebanese cuisine to the Auburn neighbourhood we call home.

Our chefs have mastered the dishes Lebanon is loved for, especially the hot and cold mezza that fill our tables. From breakfast to a late charcoal feast, we cook the way we cook for our own family - generously, and with heart.
