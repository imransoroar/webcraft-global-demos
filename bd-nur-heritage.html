A planning house for the city Dhaka is becoming.

How We Plan

Every project begins as a masterplan, not a plot.

Before a single boundary is marked we plan the whole community: road hierarchy, utilities, drainage, civic cores and reserved green. It is slower. It is also why our developments hold their value and our handovers stay clean.

  • 01Land assembly with verified, litigation-free titles and survey-grade boundaries.
  • 02In-house master-planning of roads, plots, utilities and civic zones.
  • 03Phased infrastructure delivery before plot and unit handover.
  • 04Registered transfer with full legal documentation at completion.
