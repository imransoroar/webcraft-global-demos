Welcome to Zain Alsham

Damascus Hospitality, in Every Detail

Zain Alsham is a dining destination devoted to authentic Middle Eastern and Syrian cuisine. Step through our doors and you will find a warm, welcoming room that reflects the rich cultural heritage of the Levant, where spices are toasted by hand, bread arrives fresh and warm, and no one ever leaves hungry.

Our kitchen still cooks the dishes of old Sham: slow fatteh layered with ghee and pine nuts, charcoal grills perfumed with garlic, and sweets pulled hot from the tray. Whether it is a quiet dinner for two or a feast for fifty, we set the table the way our grandmothers taught us.

"Ahlan wa sahlan, you are family here."
