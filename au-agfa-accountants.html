Register of Services

IItem

GST and BAS

We prepare and lodge Business Activity Statements to the ATO on a monthly, quarterly or annual basis across many industries - property developers, builders, formwork, IT, security, motor dealers, hospitality, fuel, wholesale and retail, Family Day Care, Uber and taxi operators.

IIItem

Tax Returns

Our certified tax agents bring more than ten years of experience lodging every kind of statement and return, including BAS, PSAR and TPAR, as well as ITR, CTR, TTR and SMSF returns, with accuracy and the best lawful outcome in mind.

IIIItem

Payroll and STP

We manage all your payroll responsibilities and compliance obligations, including superannuation, PAYG withholding and Single Touch Payroll. Cloud based software keeps reporting simple and communication with our team effortless.

IVItem

Tax Consultancy

We work to reduce tax and maximise refunds lawfully. Advice covers tax planning, assessment of income and allowable deductions, capital gains, rental property, GST, PAYG and the most suitable business structure for your circumstances.

VItem

Accounting and Bookkeeping

Cloud based bookkeeping you can access from anywhere, quick, affordable and professional, with no prior knowledge required. We work in QuickBooks and Xero and prepare complete financial statements for our business clients.

VIItem

Self Managed Super Funds

A full range of SMSF services, from establishing a new fund and registering for an ABN, TFN and GST, to setting up a corporate trustee, preparing annual financial statements and returns, member statements and arranging the independent audit required by the ATO.

VIIItem

Business Setup

We help you analyse a business you intend to buy or start and establish the right structure for your situation - companies, trusts, business name, ABN, TFN and GST or PAYG registration. We also maintain your records with the ATO and ASIC and assist with loans and accountant's letters.
