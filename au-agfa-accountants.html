The Practice

A traditional accountancy with a modern ledger.

We believe sound accounting is built on patience, precision and a long view of your affairs.

AGFA Accountants, also known as AgfaTax, is a public accountancy practice and a member of the Institute of Public Accountants. From offices in Bankstown and Tamworth, we advise individuals, families and businesses throughout New South Wales on the full breadth of their tax and financial obligations.

Our work is methodical by design. Every return is reviewed, every figure reconciled, and every obligation tracked against the calendar so that nothing is left to the final week. We combine the discipline of established practice with cloud accounting in Xero and QuickBooks, giving you both rigour and a clear, current picture of where you stand.

Whether you need a single tax return prepared with care or a complete bookkeeping and advisory arrangement for your enterprise, you will find the same steady hand and the same plain English explanations behind every recommendation.

What We Attend To

A full register of accounting disciplines.

From day to day compliance to considered strategy, our services are arranged to carry a client from first registration through to a settled, well managed set of accounts.

I

GST & BAS

Accurate Business Activity Statements prepared and lodged on time, with GST positions checked and reconciled each period.

II

Tax Returns

Individual, sole trader, company and trust returns, prepared to claim every entitlement you are properly due.

III

Payroll & STP

Reliable payroll runs with Single Touch Payroll reporting, so your staff are paid correctly and the ATO is kept current.

IV

Tax Consultancy

Forward looking advice on structure, timing and obligations, helping you plan rather than simply react at year end.

V

Accounting & Bookkeeping

Day to day bookkeeping and management accounts in Xero and QuickBooks, kept tidy, reconciled and report ready.

VI

Self-Managed Super Funds

SMSF administration, compliance and reporting, handled with the diligence your retirement savings deserve.

VII

Business Setup

Company, partnership and sole trader registrations established correctly from the outset, with ABN, GST and structure in order.

Considered Advice
