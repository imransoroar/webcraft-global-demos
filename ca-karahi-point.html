Fresh From The Karahi

No Pre-Made Curry. Just The Wok.

Karahi is not a sauce you ladle out of a pot. It is a method. Bone-in meat seared hard on high flame, tomatoes broken down in their own juices, green chilli, ginger julienne and crushed spice finished right in front of the fire. That sizzle on your table is the whole point.

  • 01

    High-Flame Sear

    Meat hits the screaming-hot iron first to lock in flavour before anything else goes in.

  • 02

    Tomato & Spice, Built Fresh

    Whole tomatoes, garlic, ginger and our ground masala cooked down to order, never batch-made.

  • 03

    Finished & Served Sizzling

    Topped with ginger, green chilli and coriander, brought to your table still bubbling in the karahi.
