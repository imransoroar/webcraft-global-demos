Who we are

A developer Dhaka families and landowners trust.

Founded in 1995, UDDL has grown into one of the capital's most established real estate developers. We design and build apartment homes and condominiums, and we partner with landowners to unlock the full value of their land.

As part of Pandughar Group, we bring institutional discipline to every project: clear planning, sound engineering and on-time delivery that owners and residents can rely on.

  • More than 150 projects handed over across Dhaka
  • Transparent joint venture terms for landowners
  • Engineering and quality standards on every floor
