Who We Are

Envisioning the growing need for secure, comfortable and dignified homes in a rapidly expanding Dhaka, Urban began its journey in 1995 under the vision of entrepreneur Nazim Uddin Ahmed.

Popularly known as Urban, the company has created lasting value for its clients, landowners and stakeholders, growing into one of the leading institutions of the industry. To date Urban has completed 125+ projects in the most prime locations of Dhaka, and over 95% of these projects have come through references from existing landowners, a clear measure of the faith placed in the company.

Urban has delivered a total of 4.4 million square feet, with a gross development value exceeding 8,000 crore BDT, a meaningful contribution to the skyline of the capital. Quality, commitment and professionalism remain the core beliefs ingrained in everything Urban builds.
