The Studio

SEC. 01 / ABOUT

Founded in 2018, Geometric Architect and Interiors (GAI) is a Dhaka based practice built on a simple discipline: every line should have a reason. We treat plans, sections and details as instruments, drawing each project the way an engineer drafts a structure, so the finished space behaves exactly as intended.

From residences and corporate offices to showrooms, restaurants and hospitals, we move a brief from first sketch to handover. Our turnkey interior fit-outs cover the full path: concept, drawings, costing, procurement and on-site execution, delivered nationwide across Bangladesh.

"Design that Makes Sense" is not a slogan. It is how we work. Sensible geometry, honest materials, and details that hold up under daily use.

From the Director

SEC. 05 / NOTE
Architect Hasnat Munim Managing Director / GAI

"A good drawing answers a question before anyone asks it on site. That is the standard we hold every project to."

When I started Geometric Architect and Interiors in 2018, I wanted a studio where design decisions could always be explained, never decoration for its own sake. Every plan we hand over is meant to make sense to the client, the contractor and, most of all, the people who will use the space every day.

We keep our team close to the work, from the first survey to the final coat of paint. Whether it is a single residence in Dhaka or a turnkey fit-out elsewhere in Bangladesh, the promise is the same: clear thinking, honest detailing, and a build that holds up. If that is the kind of partner you are looking for, I would be glad to talk.

Hasnat Munim
