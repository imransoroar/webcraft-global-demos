The practice

A studio built on measured lines and resolved detail. Every project begins with the grid and ends at handover.

Founded in 2018, Geometric Architect and Interiors (GAI) is an architecture and interior fit-out firm based in Mohammadpur, Dhaka. We treat architecture and interiors as a single continuous discipline, from the structural envelope to the last cabinet hinge.

Our method is deliberately technical. We work in plan, section and elevation, resolve circulation early, and hold the modular grid through to construction. The result is space that reads as calm, exact and built to last.

We deliver turnkey across Bangladesh, coordinating design, procurement and site so that one accountable team carries a project from first sketch to a finished, occupied room.

Method, in four phases

We work the way an engineer reads a drawing: in order, with each phase signed off before the next begins.

01·

Brief & Survey

We measure the site, study constraints and set the program, budget and the modular grid the project will hold to.

02·

Concept & Drawings

Plans, sections and elevations resolve circulation and form. You see the space in 2D and 3D before a wall moves.

03·

Detail & Procure

Construction documents, finishes and joinery are detailed, then materials and trades are sourced and scheduled.

04·

Build & Handover

Our team manages the site to specification, supervises quality, and hands over a finished, occupied space.
