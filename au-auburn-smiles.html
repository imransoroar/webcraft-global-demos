What we do

One practice for every stage of your smile

From routine care to cosmetic transformations, our Auburn team handles it all under one roof, with clear pricing explained before we begin.

Check-ups and cleans

Thorough exams, professional scale and clean, plus tailored advice to keep problems away before they start.

Children's dentistry

Friendly, unhurried visits that help little ones feel safe and build healthy habits from their very first tooth.

Cosmetic dentistry

Subtle, natural enhancements designed around your face, so your smile looks like the best version of you.

Teeth whitening

Safe, dentist-supervised whitening in-chair or take-home, for a brighter shade without the sensitivity surprises.

Clear aligners

Discreet, removable aligners that straighten teeth gradually, mapped out with a digital plan from day one.

Veneers

Hand-finished porcelain veneers to reshape, brighten and restore chipped or worn front teeth.

Dental implants

Long-lasting replacements for missing teeth that look, feel and function like the real thing.

Root canal therapy

Comfortable, modern treatment that relieves pain and saves a tooth that would otherwise be lost.

Wisdom teeth removal

Carefully planned extractions with clear aftercare, keeping you comfortable before, during and after.
