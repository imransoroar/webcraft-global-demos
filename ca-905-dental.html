Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Do I still need checkups if my teeth feel fine?

Yes. Regular checkups allow small problems to be detected early, before they escalate into larger ones.

Can children get treatment at your clinic?

Yes. Children can receive checkups, cleanings and preventive care in a welcoming, family-friendly environment.

Is sedation available for anxious patients?

Sedation options may help patients feel more comfortable during treatment. Your dentist will explain suitable options based on your needs.

Do you participate in the Canadian Dental Care Plan?

Our office participates in the CDCP. We balance bill according to the current ODA fee guide, and patients are responsible for any portion not covered by the plan.

What should I do during a dental emergency?

Contact us promptly so your condition can be evaluated as soon as possible. Early care may help prevent the problem from becoming more serious.
