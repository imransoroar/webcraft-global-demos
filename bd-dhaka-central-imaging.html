Imaging Modalities

A full range of world-class imaging under one roof.

Staffed by a board certified radiologist and credentialed technical staff, our department delivers the complete line of specialty imaging the community relies on.

3 Tesla MRI

High field magnetic resonance imaging for fine soft tissue, neuro, spine and musculoskeletal detail without radiation.

3T · High resolution

256 Slice CT Scan

Multi slice real time computed tomography for fast, low dose cross sectional studies including angiography.

256 slice · Real time

Ultrasound & Doppler

USG 2D and 4D, Echo Color Doppler, echocardiogram and duplex study for vascular, cardiac and obstetric imaging.

USG 2D / 4D · Color Doppler

Digital X-Ray & CR

1000 MA digital X-ray with computed radiography, plain dental X-ray and OPG for crisp, instantly available films.

1000 MA · OPG / Dental

Mammography & BMD

Dedicated mammography for breast screening and bone mineral density scanning for osteoporosis assessment.

Mammography · BMD

Cardiac & Neuro Studies

12 channel ECG, ETT, Holter monitor, digital EEG and NCV/EMG for complete cardiac and neurophysiology workups.

ECG · EEG · NCV/EMG
