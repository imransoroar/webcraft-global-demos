The Charcoal Difference

Fire, Smoke & Patience

There is no shortcut to a great kebab. We start with fresh halal meat, hand it our house marinade of garlic, paprika and Mediterranean spice, then leave it to rest overnight so every skewer carries real flavour.

When the order lands, it goes over glowing charcoal, not a flat grill. That open flame is where the smoke, the char and the honest grill-house taste come from.

  • Real Charcoal HeatOpen flame char on every skewer and chop
  • Overnight MarinadeGarlic, paprika and spice soaked in deep
  • Made To OrderNothing sits under a heat lamp, ever
