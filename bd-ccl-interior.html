How we build it

04 / Process
STEP 01

Listen and frame

We sit with you, walk the site and understand how the space needs to perform before any concept appears.

STEP 02

Concept and 3D

Layouts, mood, materials and lighting come together in clear visuals so you can see the room before it exists.

STEP 03

Detail and source

Working drawings, finishes and trusted Dhaka vendors are locked so nothing is left to chance on site.

STEP 04

Deliver and hand over

We supervise execution to the final coat, then hand over a space that matches the drawing exactly.
