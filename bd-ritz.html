The Experience

A table at RITZ is a slow evening, measured in courses, candlelight and conversation that need not be rushed.

Seeds and Scions began with a simple belief: that fine dining in Dhaka should feel personal. Every plate is built from carefully sourced produce, every pour considered, every setting laid by hand. The room seats few, by design, so that service can be attentive and the evening yours.

Continental technique guides the kitchen, while gentle notes of South Asian spice and seasonal fusion keep each menu rooted in where we are. The result is a destination dining room, warm, deliberate and unmistakably ours.
