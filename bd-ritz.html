The Experience

Dining as an evening, not an errand.

We built RITZ for the long table and the slow course. Seven floors above Road 111, the dining room is dim, gilded, and intimate, the kind of room that makes an ordinary Tuesday feel like an occasion.

Our kitchen works in a continental hand with a Bengal accent. Produce arrives each morning, sauces are reduced through the afternoon, and nothing leaves the pass until it is exactly right. Seeds and Scions has always meant the same thing here: roots, and what grows from them.

Reserve the window for two, or the chef's counter for an unhurried tasting. Either way, expect to stay a while.
