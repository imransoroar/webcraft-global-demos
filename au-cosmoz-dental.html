The Philosophy

The art of the smile

A smile is the most personal thing you wear. At Cosmoz Dental Care we treat cosmetic dentistry as a craft, shaping every veneer, contour and shade to suit your face, your character and the light you live in.

An established, family-friendly practice a short walk from Auburn station, we pair the comfort of relaxation with the precision of fine work. Free patient parking is available in our building.

  • i.Designed around your face, never a template
  • ii.Gentle, unhurried treatment for adults and children
  • iii.Natural materials, natural-looking results
  • iv.All major health funds and HICAPS on the spot
