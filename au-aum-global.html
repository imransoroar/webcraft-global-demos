A visa decision turns on the detail. Our practice is built to get that detail correct before it is ever lodged.

The Australian visa system is intricate, and small errors in evidence, timing or eligibility can decide an outcome. At AUM Global we take the stress out of that process by treating every application as a case to be prepared properly, not a form to be filled in.

Whether you are a skilled professional, a partner joining a loved one, a student, or an employer sponsoring a worker, our registered agents assess your circumstances honestly, set out the realistic pathway, and prepare a submission designed to stand up to scrutiny.

And when an application has been refused, we are equally at home on the other side of the decision, representing clients through review and appeal.
