The Firm

A multi-city practice built on precision and patience.

Migration law rewards detail. A single missed clause, an unsigned form or a weak statement can cost months. AUM Global was founded to give applicants the rigour of a large firm without the cold distance of one.

From offices in Parramatta, Sydney CBD and our head office in Melbourne, we manage every stage in house: eligibility, documentation, lodgement and the difficult conversations that follow a refusal. Hundreds of clients have trusted us to move their lives across borders, and our reviews speak to the care behind each file.

We do not promise outcomes the regulations cannot deliver. We promise an honest reading of your chances and a plan written for your circumstances, not a template.

Our Strength

A refusal letter is a turning point, not a full stop.

Most agents lodge applications. Fewer are willing to take on the harder work that begins after a decision goes against you. This is where AUM Global has built its name.

We read the delegate's reasons line by line, identify what was missed or misjudged, and rebuild the case for the Administrative Appeals Tribunal with the evidence and submissions a review demands.

  • Refusal review and merits assessmentAn honest reading of whether the decision can be challenged, before you spend a dollar on review.
  • AAT and tribunal representationLodgement within the strict deadlines, fresh evidence, written submissions and hearing preparation.
  • Visa cancellation responsesTime-critical replies to notices of intention to cancel, protecting the status you already hold.
