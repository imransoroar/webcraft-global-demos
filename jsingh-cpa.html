What we do

Full-service accounting & tax for people and businesses.

From a single personal return to complete back-office support for your company — everything you need under one roof.

Personal Tax (T1)

Maximize your refund and stay compliant with accurate personal returns.

  • T1 personal income tax returns
  • Self-employed & rental income
  • Credits, deductions & prior-year filings

Small Business & Corporate Tax

Tax planning and filing that keeps your company efficient and compliant.

  • T2 corporate income tax returns
  • Incorporation & company setup
  • Year-end financial statements

Bookkeeping

Clean, organized books so you always know where your business stands.

  • Monthly & quarterly bookkeeping
  • Bank & credit-card reconciliation
  • QuickBooks & cloud accounting setup

Payroll

Reliable payroll so your team is paid correctly and on time, every time.

  • Payroll processing & remittances
  • T4 / T5 slip preparation
  • ROE filing & source deductions

HST / GST

Registration and filings handled so you stay current with sales tax.

  • HST/GST registration
  • Periodic HST/GST returns
  • Input tax credit review

CRA Support & Consulting

An accountant in your corner when the CRA comes calling — and for planning ahead.

  • CRA reviews, audits & correspondence
  • WSIB & EHT compliance
  • Business advisory & tax planning
