Meet Dr. Sandhu

For years, Dr. Sashpal Sandhu has built his Brampton practice on a simple idea: there is no one-size-fits-all approach to dental care. Every patient gets a treatment plan made just for them, explained in plain language, with no pressure and no rush.

Dr. Sandhu and his team work as one, dedicated to improving and maintaining your oral health for the long run. Whatever your dental needs, you will be in great hands.

  • Individualized care, never a rushed appointment
  • Comfortable, stress-free visits with modern tools
  • All your family's dental needs under one roof
What we do

Caring for every part of your smile

From routine cleanings to a full smile makeover, here is how we can help, explained simply.

Preventive & Family Care

Dental exams, cleanings, X-rays, sealants, night guards and TMJ care to keep every family member healthy.

Dental Implants

Single implants, bridges, implant-supported and removable dentures to restore missing teeth comfortably.

Orthodontics

Traditional metal or clear braces, Invisalign, and early treatment for children to guide growing smiles.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Tooth-coloured fillings, teeth whitening, veneers, laminates and bonding for a brighter, confident smile.

Crowns, Bridges & Root Canal

Custom-fitted crowns and bridges, plus gentle root canal therapy to save and protect your natural teeth.

Emergency & Same-Day Care

Toothache, broken or knocked-out tooth, jaw injury? Reach us by phone 7 days a week for urgent attention.
