Our Story

A delightful tale,
told in flavour.

Hongbao is a family of ardent epicures with a single, simple dream: to explore far and wide and bring the most palate-friendly delicacies of the East home to Dhaka. Each plate carries a distinct quality that kindles curiosity, quenched by a single bite.

Here, the elements complement one another to create a rhapsody of culinary artistry, careful attention to detail, and high-quality ingredients sourced from across the world. Step into a world where contemporary Chinese aesthetics meet the evolving spirit of East Asian cuisine.

Chef Kushal Lama

Executive Chef, Hongbao Dhaka
"My culinary journey began at Mainland China and grew through leading roles at Marriott and Yauatcha - training teams, launching menus and building kitchens that consistently deliver excellence."

At Hongbao, Chef Lama channels years of fine dining mastery into a kitchen devoted to the evolving East. Every dim sum is hand-folded to order, every sauce balanced with restraint, and every signature designed to surprise the palate while honouring tradition.
