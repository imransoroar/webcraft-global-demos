Meet Haisam.

"I started this firm because clients deserve an accountant who actually knows their name, their business and what keeps them up at night."

Raad Accountants and Advisors is built around Haisam Radd, and that is the whole point. This is not a factory of files and faceless portals. It is a boutique practice in the heart of the Bankstown and Greenacre area, where every client gets Haisam's direct attention and a relationship that lasts well beyond a single tax return.

Whether you are a tradie with one truck, a growing family business, or a household trying to make sense of super and structure, Haisam translates the complicated into plain language and a clear plan.
